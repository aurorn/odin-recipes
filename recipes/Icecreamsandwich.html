Ice Cream Sandwich

Want an ice cream sandwich but you only have biscuits and ice cream seperately? Well this guide you how to make your own ice cream sandwich!

Ingredient List:

  • Biscuits
  • Ice Cream

How to make Ice Cream Sandwich:

  1. Grab a plate
  2. Place 2 Biscuits on the plate
  3. Scoop out some Ice cream and place it on one of the biscuits
  4. Place the other biscuit on top of the ice cream
  5. Eat the Ice cream sandwich
