Mac and Cheese

This is a recipe for a delicious Mac and Cheese that beats the boxed kind any day of the week.

Ingredient List:

  • Macaroni: 8 ounces of uncooked Elbow Macaroni
  • Butter: 1/4 cup of salted butter
  • Flour: 3 tablespoons of All-Purpose Flour
  • Milk: 2 1/2 cups of milk, or more as needed
  • Cheeses: 2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese and 1/2 cup of finely grated Parmesan cheese
  • Seasonings: Salt and pepper to taste

Bread Crumb Topping:

  • Bread Crumbs: 1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
  • Butter: 2 tablespoons of salted butter
  • Paprika: 1 pinch of ground paprika

How to make Mac and Cheese:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8-inch Square baking dish

  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the macaroni and simmer, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes; it will finish cookng in the oven. Drain and transfer to the prepared baking dish.

  3. While the macaroni is cooking, melt 1/4 cup of butter in a medium skillet over low heat. Whisk in flour and stir until the mixture becomes paste-like and light golden brown, 3 to 5 minutes.

  4. Gradually whisk in the 2 1/2 cups milk into the flour mixture and bring to a simmer. Stir in the shredded Cheddar and finely grated Parmesan cheeses; season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook and stir over low heat until cheese is melted and sauce has thickened, 3 to 5 minutes, adding up to 1/2 cup more milk if needed. Pour the cheese sauce over macaroni and stir until well combined.

  5. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add bread crumbs; cook and stir until well coated and browned. Spread bread crumbs over macaroni and cheese, then sprinkle with paprika.

  6. Bake int he preheated oven until topping is golden brown and macaroni and cheese is bubbling, about 30 minutes.
